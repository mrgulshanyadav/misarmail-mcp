# Campaign Strategy

Generate a complete email campaign strategy including subject lines, send schedule, audience segmentation, and A/B test plan.

## Arguments
- **goal** (required): Campaign objective (e.g., "product launch", "re-engagement", "newsletter growth")
- **audience** (optional): Target audience description
- **timeline** (optional): Campaign duration

## Example
"Create a 3-email re-engagement campaign for users who haven't opened emails in 30 days"
