# Subject Line Optimizer

Generate AI-optimized email subject lines with predicted open rate scores and A/B test recommendations.

## Arguments
- **topic** (required): Campaign topic or content summary
- **tone** (optional): Writing tone (professional, casual, urgent, playful, informative)
- **count** (optional): Number of suggestions (default: 5)

## Example
"Generate 5 subject lines for our Black Friday sale announcement"
